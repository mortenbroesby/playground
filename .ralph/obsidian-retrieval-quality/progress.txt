## Codebase Patterns
- Add only reusable patterns here.
- `tools/obsidian-memory` now supports append-only retrieval events in `.rag/retrieval-events.jsonl`; search/context emit weak-use entries and unfold emits strong-use entries keyed by chunk metadata.
- `lexical-index.json` is now schema version 3 with per-field postings and document stats that can back BM25/BM25F-style ranking.
- `rag:query` and the MCP search surface both understand `default` vs `quality` retrieval modes, with quality widening the candidate pool without changing the default path.
- `tools/obsidian-memory/src/rag-retrieval-evals.mjs` is the dedicated judged retrieval-evals surface; it replays representative typed fixture queries across default and quality retrieval modes so later fusion tuning can extend one canonical harness.
